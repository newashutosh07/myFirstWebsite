Early Life

Roseanne Park (born February 11, 1997), better known as Rose, is a South Korean singer and dancer. She was born in Auckland, New Zealand, and raised in Melbourne, Australia. Rose became interested in music at a young age and began playing guitar and singing in high school.

Blackpink

Rose joined Blackpink in 2016, which is a four-member girl group formed by YG Entertainment. She is the main vocalist of the group and is known for her unique voice and emotional performances. Blackpink has become one of the most successful girl groups in the world, breaking records with their music videos and topping charts with their albums

Solo Career

Rose made her solo debut in 2021 with the single album "R". The album includes the title track "On The Ground", which topped charts in South Korea and internationally. Rose's solo career showcases her musical versatility and personal style.
